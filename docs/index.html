What gets filtered

5 filters. All free. All local.

Every filter runs as a pattern-matching rule in your browser. No API calls, no subscriptions required for the core experience.

How it works

Installs in 30 seconds. Works silently.

No account. No setup. No data sent anywhere.

1

Add to Chrome

One click from the Chrome Web Store. Free.

2

Open LinkedIn

The extension activates automatically on linkedin.com.

3

Posts are filtered

As your feed loads, matching posts are silently hidden or collapsed.

4

Tune your filters

Click the extension icon to toggle filters, add custom words, or block accounts.
